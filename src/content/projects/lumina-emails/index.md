A three-email sequence for **Lumina**, a haircare brand, designed to take a new
subscriber from first impression to first purchase. Each email is designed and
written around a single job.

## The strategy

The sequence follows a deliberate arc rather than three disconnected sends:

1. **Email 1 — Welcome & promise.** Introduce the brand, set the tone, and earn the
   next open. The goal is trust and a reason to keep reading, not a hard sell.
2. **Email 2 — Educate & agitate.** Speak to the specific problem the reader came for,
   make it concrete, and position the product as the considered solution.
3. **Email 3 — Convert.** A focused call to action with a clear offer and the proof
   needed to act, while the relationship is still warm.

## The craft

Each email leads with one idea, keeps the reader moving with short paragraphs and a
single primary CTA, and is designed to feel clean and effortless to read. The previews
below show all three in sequence.
